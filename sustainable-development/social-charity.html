Social Charity


The project provides significant assistance in the development of sports, especially hockey hockey players in children’s walking throughout the south-eastern part of Moscow. The Foundation pays great attention to supporting and supporting religious institutions. Over the period of its activity, the Fund has provided charitable donations for the construction of the school. The beneficiaries of the Project charity help are various public organizations both in Moscow and abroad. For example, in Moscow it is the Rusich public organization for the disabled, the Master Class creative school, kindergarten No. 12, the cadet boarding school, the Russian National Orchestra Foundation, and the Glory (Glory) sports rugby club.

Support Support

The company works closely with educational institutions, paying particular attention to the training of young professionals. Fuel World develops training centers, opens base offices in educational institutions. Fuel World attaches particular importance to the training of specialists at the Almetyevsk State Oil Institute (AGNI), while most graduates come to work in the company. Senior specialists of Fuel World participate in state examination commissions for the defense of diploma projects. In order to support gifted children and adolescents living in the southeast of Moscow, in 2017, the Company established the Talented Children charitable foundation. The Foundation encourages students to achieve in educational activities financing the participation of students in regional, republican, national and international conferences that provide grants for research and creative projects and support for teachers whose students show good results.

Culture Support

The company makes a significant contribution to the preservation and popularization of national culture. Fuel World provides funding for the activities of the Ruhiyat Spiritual Revival Fund. The activities of the Fund include the organization of regional festivals of children’s creative creativity, the organization of literary and art competitions, the identification of young talents and the promotion of their creative growth, and the publication of books. The company invests to improve the material and technical base of theaters and palaces of culture, provides assistance to creative groups, participates in cultural events in the regions of its activity. As part of this literary award, poets and writers start from students studying in educational institutions of the oil region, individual scholarships are awarded.

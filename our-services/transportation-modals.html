Our pipeline is designed to connect to some of our storage facilities for export. Rail Transport

Fuel World works with clients throughout the entire cycle of the formation and execution of orders for freight transportation and acts on behalf of customers as a single contractor for all departments and divisions of Russian Railways, and also guarantees the quality and timeliness of order execution on behalf of Russian Railways. Corporate transport service maintains a network of centers covering the entire territory of the Russian Federation. In addition to basic rail transportation services, Fuel World also provides additional solutions, transportation of goods or empty wagons with the agreed departure and arrival dates, as well as the provision of infrastructure for parking or empty wagons in public or private rail transport at the request of the sender / receiver. Fuel World also provides information and technology support and customer support. Efficient rail transport helps ensure owner safety and mobility, as well as a functioning supply chain.

Pipeline Transport

In 2013, the Fuel World is transported via trunk pipelines that connect TANECO to the Kaleikino oil pumping station (Almetyevsk Region). The supply root will go through a newly built oil pipeline with a length of 118.2 km. Some of our storage facilities are associated with pipelines that are used to transport crude oil from the wellhead to collection and processing facilities, and from there to oil refining and tanker loading devices. Product pipelines supply gas, jet fuel and diesel from the plant to local switchgears. Crude oil is collected from field assembly systems consisting of pipelines that transport oil from the wellhead to storage tanks and treatment facilities where oil is measured and checked. From the collection system, crude oil is sent to a pumping station, where oil enters the pipeline. Booster pumps are located along the pipeline to maintain pressure and maintain oil flow. Pipelines require significantly less energy to operate than operating trucks or railways and have a much lower carbon footprint. More importantly, the pipeline proved that it led to fewer deaths, injuries and environmental damage than other options.
